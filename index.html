About Us

Welcome to Take It Cheesy

Since our modest beginnings in 2005 with a little space in kolkata’s stylish Central locale, ‘Take it cheesy’ ‘s development has been enlivened with the energy to cook and serve solid, Indian-roused takeout food. In contrast to other Indian eateries, ‘Take it cheesy’ was made with the explicit expectation to appear as something else.

Here, ancient cultures to modern trends co-exist , and the intricacies of Indian culture blend somelessly together reflecting the depth , richness and vibrancy of every diverse culinary with fast -casual setting.Inspired by our Co-founder and Executive Chief Mr.Singhs Mission we serve the original flavored spices food .Gather round the table and you will be in for a holiday treat with the festive fete
